# Facilitator Catas

How to ruin an architecture kata before it even starts.

- [The Over-Corrector](the-over-corrector.md) Jump in at the first sign of trouble instead of letting teams learn from mistakes.
- [The Vague Briefing](the-vague-briefing.md) Skip the intro. Don't explain objectives, deliverables, or time structure.
- [The Tech-Savvy Stakeholder](the-tech-savvy-stakeholder.md) Play the business role but talk like an architect, accidentally handing teams the solution.
- [The Harsh Critic](the-harsh-critic.md) Tell teams exactly what they got wrong instead of guiding them to discover the gaps.
